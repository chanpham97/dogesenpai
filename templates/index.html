Insurance 101

Background

Our approach to understanding insurance comes from data and computers! We fed the information of 1.4M users into a neural network ("computer brain") and let it tell us which pieces of that information are most important to predict the cost of your premiums!

Some things we found

  • Top factors for determining premiums were DOB, optional insured, and people covered in plan
  • Date of Birth is the most significant feature for determining Bronze premiums
  • Height, weight, and BMI have little impact on cost of premiums
  • Location affects bronze and platinum premiums, favoring west coast for lower costs
